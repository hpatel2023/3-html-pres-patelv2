A boy who was born in a poor family,
	Didn’t have a lavish childhood,
	His first job was selling bhajias(snacks),
	Did not had a college degree,
	Had a business acumen since young age,
	Worked hard with the resources he had,
	Build a great reputation,
	Started a great business,
	Using his business mind to convince local investors,
	Made India’s biggest company.
